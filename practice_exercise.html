Spice Girls

Spice Girls are an English pop group that formed in 1994. The group comprised of five members; Sporty Spice, Scary Spice, Posh, Baby Spice and Ginger Spice. Their debut single, Wannabe, was released in 1996. Their single hit number one in 37 countries. Their debut album, Spice, sold more than 31 million copies worldwide. The popular motto from the Spice Girls is, "Girl Power!"
